Yelp: Fast Food Analysis

Analyze Yelp data to explore and find trends in the fast food restaurants across the United States. Primarily to determine if there exists a correlation between the type of cuisine served and the location (state) of the restauarant.

Timeline

January 2020 - March 2020

Tools & Technologies

Python (Seaborns, Matplotlib, Pandas, Numpy, Geopandas, Scipy), Jupyter Notebooks

Course

COGS 108: Data Science in Practice

</> Code

Takeaways

My Role

Gather data using the Yelp API, data cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis (geospatial data maps, popularity), Wrote the functions to find average ratings by cuisine for fast food Restaurants, Testing Distributions (normality of cuisine ratings).

Description

  • Top rated cuisines differed for fast food restaurant and non fast food restaurants.
    • Top 3 non fast food cuisines were Japanese, Mexican, and Mediterranean, while the top 3 fast food cuisines were Thai, Greek, and Japanese.
  • South Dakota had the most fast food restaurants for every 100,000 people.
  • Fast Food Restuarant Ratings:
    • The Halal Guys was the most popular restaurant in our dataset.
    • The Great Steak was the least popular restaurant in our dataset.

Findings

We concluded that it is very unlikely that cuisines do not affect rating of a non fast food restaurant.

We concluded that the type of cuisine of a non fast food restaurant influences the rating of a restaurant.

We concluded that the type of cuisine does influence the rating of a fast food restaurant.
